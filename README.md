#Creating a utility tool for the Dark Souls series
Purpose of the program:
    What the program is all about:
        FromSoftware created the Dark Souls series (a trilogy) and released its best game, Elden Ring, in 2022. These games are 
        world-renowned for their gameplay and the variety of character options available for completing the game. You start as 
        an ordinary person (a knight) and choose the type of warrior you want to be; as the game progresses, you upgrade their
        equipment and abilities (strength, health, intelligence, etc.), and that’s how you complete the game. But there’s a 
        problem: the game offers vast possibilities for choosing equipment and the type of character you can create, but during 
        the game you can only upgrade a limited amount of equipment and rebuild your character’s stats (abilities) just once; 
        moreover, this requires resources you might not have, and that’s why we came up with the idea of creating a program 
        where you can design and predict a character’s stats without limitations for all 4 games.
    How the program works: 
        On the home screen, you select which game you want to create a character for (there are 4, and they’re all unique in
        their own way). After that, you’re taken to the page for that game, where you can enter any character stats, choose any 
        weapons, armor, and accessories (a very important part of the game!) and see the result: how much damage you can deal 
        to enemies, what defense you’ll have against their attacks and magic, what your load will be (also an important part of 
        the game), and so on. After that, you’ll be able to save your character so you can come back later to check what you 
        need to improve further in the game itself. This will apply to 4 games that are similar in concept but unique in design 
        (for each game, the character creation design will be unique to the game’s theme), weapon sets, armor, accessories, and 
        character upgrades.
        
PS. Weapons have a unique scaling property based on selected character stats (Strength, Agility, Intelligence); there’s a
special formula that, depending on the level of a specific ability, provides a damage boost to the damage your hero deals to
enemies. These are represented in a ranking system (D-S), where “D” is the lowest damage boost and “S” is the highest.
